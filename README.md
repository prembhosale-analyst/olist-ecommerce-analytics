**# Olist E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard**

## Project Overview
End-to-end data analytics project analyzing 100K+ orders from 
Olist, Brazil's largest e-commerce platform (2016–2018).

## Tools Used
- Power BI (Dashboard & Data Modeling)
- DAX (Measures & Calculations)
- Excel (Data Cleaning)
- SQL (Exploratory Analysis)

## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle — Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist
- 9 tables | 99,441 orders | 32,951 products | 3,095 sellers

## Key Findings
- Total Revenue: R$15.84M across 99K orders
- Health & Beauty is the top category at R$1.44M revenue
- August is the peak revenue month at R$2.19M
- Average review score: 4.09 / 5.0
- SP (São Paulo) dominates with highest customer concentration
